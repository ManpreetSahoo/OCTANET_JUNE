SOME AYURVEDIC TREES AND ITS BENEFITS

Giloy

It is an herb used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine, boosts immunity, helps in managing chronic fever, and has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It's also known to improve digestion and manage blood sugar levels.

Saffron

It is a valuable spice, is known for its antioxidant properties, enhancing mood and memory, and supporting heart health. It may also aid in reducing PMS symptoms and promoting skin health.

Globe Artichoke

It is rich in antioxidants and supports liver health by promoting bile production. It also aids digestion, lowers cholesterol levels, and can improve heart health.

Ashwagandha

It is an adaptogenic herb, reduces stress and anxiety, and enhances mental clarity and energy levels. It also supports immune function, improves sleep quality, and may boost muscle strength and endurance.

Milk thistle

It is known for its liver-protective properties, aiding in detoxification and supporting liver health. It also has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, potentially improving skin health and blood sugar levels.

Yellow Gentian

It is valued for its digestive benefits, stimulating appetite and alleviating indigestion. It also has anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help support overall gastrointestinal health.
